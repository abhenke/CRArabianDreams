Counseling / Lessons / Training.

This is for people with "problem" horses and beginners. I help either here or on site with the owner and horse. For problem horses, I help figure out what the "problem" is by talking with the owner and watching the horse and owner work together. I help both the horse and owner by giving them the knowledge of "reading" each others body language so they better understand each other. I will help find each of their strengths and weakness to be able to be a successful team on the trail or any events they are involved in. For beginners, I help build confidence for both horse and owner. I teach steps in training. I also help with learning about equipment, cost, feed, care, and maintenance of a horse.

Breeding/Sales
