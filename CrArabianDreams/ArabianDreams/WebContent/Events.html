Open House

April 25, 2020 at 9 AM – April 26, 2020 at 6 PM
Free Event
Smoked meats and Beverages

Come meet the horses! Princess Charlie will love the extra attention! If you are new to the equine world, or thinking about getting involved, what better place to start?! We will provide lists of clubs, Barns, and fun events that you can participate in. Learn options available to you before you invest in a purchase. Learn what to look for in your search for the perfect equine. Meet other equine enthusiast that you can learn from and with. Get an overall idea of what will be involved in cost, equipment and care of getting a equine partner. Be able to interact with some of the Cr Dreams equine friends to get a "feel" of what you like and what may or may not work for you in personality and gender. this will be a two day free event. Take a quick look around or stick around and visit all are welcome!! Feel free to message me with any questions, concerns or Ideas you may have. Looking forward to seeing you!!
