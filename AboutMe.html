Lessons

Offered for people looking into horse ownership, learn about cost, care, and what to look for in a future equine partner, and basic riding. I also offer lessons for those who would like to simply be around horses and learn more about them. These are basic riding lessons, which include how to handle the horse on the ground, saddling, and learning about the equipment being used.
- I do not do show riding -

Counseling / Training.

This is for people with "problem" horses. I help either here or on site with the owner and the horse. I figure out what the "problem" is by talking with the owner and watching the horse and owner working together. I help both the horse and owner by giving them the knowledge of "reading" each others body language so they better understand each other. I will help find the horses' and owners' strengths and weakness to be a successful team on the trail or any other events they choose to be involved in.
$150 - $200 - $350
1-2 1/2 day
People provisions, and stall provided.
